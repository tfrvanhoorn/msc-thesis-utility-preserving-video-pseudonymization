# Storyline Thesis

1. **Why interesting?**
     
    (a) Automatic human action recognition is a rapidly advancing field of AI with crucial applications in sensitive, high-stakes domains like medicine and forensics.

    (b) However, using video data in these domains involves processing personal and sensitive information, making privacy preservation a requirement.

    (c) Some critical applications require longitudinal analysis, which necessitates a stable identity, mapping an individual to a consistent anonymized identity across different frames and videos.

    (d) Developing an approach that preserves utility while ensuring consistency and privacy protection will ethically unlock the potential for large-scale video AI analysis in these critical domains.
